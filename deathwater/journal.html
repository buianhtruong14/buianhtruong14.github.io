Water is being destroyed and dying over time by humans

Today, our rivers, streams, reservoirs, lakes and the sea are gradually polluted with chemicals, waste, plastics and lots of other pollutants. This is the reason why water sources are seriously polluted, negatively affecting human health as well as animals and plants due to water pollution.

Water pollution is quite common in Vietnam and negatively affects the health of the people. Water is indirectly killing more people every year and is on the rise with time. In Vietnam, the natural water source to drink and not harm the body is almost zero. If we don't take action, our clean water will be used in the future. quite a lot now but we need to dig deeper first.

Firstly, the management of imported waste still has many loopholes. According to the General Department of Customs, as of August 28, 2018, the whole country had up to 17,000 containers of scrap without clearance, mainly at some seaports. These wastes are mainly plastic, paper, iron and aluminum scrap brought by foreign shipping lines. In urban areas and many provinces, landfills are not treated due to landfills and overburden disposal. The amount of solid waste such as plastic, plastic bags, ... is increasing and dumping into the sea and river, causing serious pollution in some areas.

The above mentioned serious environmental pollution are caused by objective and subjective reasons, of which subjective is the main reason.

Regarding objective reasons: Our country is located in a geographical location greatly affected by climate change, leading to drought, flood and salinity in some areas. Budget conditions are very difficult, so the investment in the environment is very limited. Rapid population growth, urbanization and industrialization also contribute to increasing greenhouse gas emissions, degradation, resource depletion and biodiversity loss ....

On subjective reasons: Awareness and awareness of environmental protection responsibility of a large number of officials, public servants, civil servants and people on vironmental protection have gradually changed. In particular, the sense of responsibility of part of government officials at all levels and business owners is still limited. Some officials governed by local interests violate environmental protection laws. The state management, law enforcement and application of science and technology in environmental protection in many places have not been paid enough attention.
