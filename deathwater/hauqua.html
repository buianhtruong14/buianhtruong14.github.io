Consequences of water pollution

The common consequence of water pollution is the increasing proportion of people with acute and chronic diseases related to water pollution such as conjunctivitis, diarrhea, and cancer. People living in polluted areas are becoming increasingly infected with suspected diseases due to the use of dirty water in all activities. In addition, water pollution also causes great losses for production and business sectors and aquaculture households.

Ponds, lakes and rivers in Vietnam are seriously polluted, and people in these areas do not have enough clean water for daily use as well as for irrigation activities. It greatly affects the living conditions of people.
