Measures to overcome water pollution

Faced with these requirements, measures must be taken to protect water resources - to protect our own future.

Improve the sense of responsibility of the community

This is the most important measure. If each of us raises our sense of responsibility for water conservation, we will build a good community. Perhaps from the most trivial things such as: do not throw waste indiscriminately, do not discharge waste, toxins directly into the environment, in the process of using pesticides, plant protection products Need to use according to instructions , do not throw indiscriminately into the environment, especially the water environment.

Save water

While clean water is increasingly polluted, our mission is to save water. In the process of using daily water such as eating, bathing, cleaning activities ... using enough water, turning off the faucet when not in use, limiting washing food directly under the tap ...

Waste treatment before being discharged into the environment

Waste treatment before being discharged into the environment

Daily-life wastes, agricultural and industrial wastes, before being discharged into the environment, must be properly and appropriately treated. Waste classification is a necessary activity. Or it is necessary to build wastewater treatment systems in industrial areas, and strictly treat according to regulations before being discharged into the environment.

In addition to the above measures, many families now use water filters to protect their own health. This is the safest and easiest way to take it for all families.

Water filters help provide clean water for drinking, living, ensuring the best quality water supply. Depending on the needs, you can choose a water filter that is compatible with the needs of each family.

To serve the general purpose of the whole family, the total domestic water filter can be used. If only used for dining, cooking, you should use a household water filter. If only purely for bathing purposes.
